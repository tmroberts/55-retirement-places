The Cottages at Lyndhurst is an intimate 55+ community located in Keller, Texas. With just 20 attached homes, The Cottages at Lyndhurst is the perfect active adult community for residents who want a close-knit neighborhood where everyone knows each other while being able to enjoy the beautiful year-round Northern Texas climate. Although there is no clubhouse, The Cottages at Lyndhurst offers homeowners the chance to enjoy the Northern Texas climate. Scenic courtyards with fountains are clustered between homes and sidewalks link neighbors together, creating a natural meeting point for residents.

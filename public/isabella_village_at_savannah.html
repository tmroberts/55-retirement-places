Situated within the master-planned community of Savannah, Isabella Village is a 55+ age-restricted neighborhood. Residents have full access to all the extensive recreational features of Savannah's multi-age environment. These include a clubhouse, fitness complex, sports courts, lakes, and picnic areas. The 20,000 square-foot Club Savannah has an elegant ballroom with spacious ceilings, library, conference room. Residents have use of the fitness center containing weight room, saunas, cardiovascular exercise room. The community grounds include a covered basketball court, sand volleyball court, four regulation tennis courts, picnic areas, and five fishing lakes.

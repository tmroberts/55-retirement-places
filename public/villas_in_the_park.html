Villas in the Park is an active lifestyle community located in Fairview, TX. A community of 227 homes, Villas in the Park has single-family and attached homes that are available on a resale-only basis. Villas in the Park appeals to active adults and retirees because of its low-maintenance lifestyle, wonderful amenities, modest homes, and community activities and events. At the center of the community is the clubhouse. The clubhouse features a multi-purpose room for the neighborhood. The community has a well-equipped exercise room, outdoor pool & patio, biking trails, beautiful parks, a scenic pond and gazebo.
